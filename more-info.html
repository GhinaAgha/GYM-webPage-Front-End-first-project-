How Our Journey Began

An experience like no other!

The Monday Fitness concept came to light from a deep passion for fitness and wellness. Within Beirut, the vibrant heart of the middle east, we opened our doors in spring 2016 and since then we welcome our peers every minute of everyday knowing that we grow with them just as much as they grow with us.

Our studio thrives on the open hearted, fitness enthusiasm of our community of proud Mondayers who see us as a sanctuary for health and fitness in all its meaning. To some we are a heaven of positivity, to others we are the dynamic hub of high-end luxury fitness, and to many we are that happy place to fulfill their soul and bodies need for growth. To us, we are all of that and much more!

Monday prides itself on its commitment to service excellence, strive for improvement, and never-ending journey to better the lives of everyone in its reach. Our community, trainers and clients alike, all stand witness to our pledge to advocate a healthier lifestyle, a stronger body and mind, and a unique path of self-care.

We welcome all who stand by our pledge, and all who wish to take the Monday path, a path to a better self, and a better community, and above all, a path to Starting Brand New!
